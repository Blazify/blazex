Grammer Rules of the Parser:

Methods                                                : Priority (Bottom to above)             

expr 						       : KEYWORD:var IDENTIFIER EQUALS expr
     						       : comp-expr ((KEYWORD:and || KEYWORD:or) compExpr())

compExpr 					       : KEYWORD:not compExpr
                                                       : arith-expr ((DOUBLE_EQUALS || LESS_THAN || GREATER_THAN || LESS_THAN_EQUALS || GREATER_THAN_EQUALS) arithExpr())

arithExpr                                              : term ((PLUS || MINUS) term())

term	                                               : factor ((MULTIPLY || DIVIDE) factor())

factor	                                               : (PLUS|MINUS) factor()
	                                               : power()

power	                                               : atom() (POWER factor)

atom 	                                               : INT || FLOAT || IDENTIFIER
	                                               : LEFT_PARENTHESIS expr RIGHT_PARENTHEIS

Returns a node (BinOpNode, UnaryOpNode, NumberNode, VarAssignNode, VarAcessNode)

For now the node is passed to the interpreter for the result but in future it'll be replaced with a compiler...
